The iterative user-research process

The first step to take is to recognize each condition in order to differentiate proposed solutions. For this reason, a process centred on people is applied as the core methodology for this research, allowing individuals who will be served by the designed outcome, to engage in the process of solutions to their issues.

“The challenge is to use the principles of human-centered design to produce positive results, products that enhance lives and add to our pleasure and enjoyment.”

Don Norman

The application of a backward path, from the ideal expectation, up to the localization of the requirements is employed to lead step-by-step to more meaningful realisations. Moreover, we tried to prototype and then develop practical solutions that can be used in real-life situations.
